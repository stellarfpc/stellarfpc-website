Who We Are

StellarFPC is a fire protection and code consulting firm focused on practical, code-compliant solutions for building owners, property managers, contractors, consultants, and design teams.

We support projects involving fire sprinkler system design, Fire Safety Plans, Ontario Fire Code consulting, and fire inspection order response. Our approach is built around clear communication, practical documentation, and a strong understanding of fire protection requirements in real buildings.
