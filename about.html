Who We Are

Practical support for owners, property managers, contractors, consultants, and design teams.

We are a practical fire protection and code consulting firm serving building owners, property managers, contractors, consultants, and design teams across Ontario.

We provide fire sprinkler system design, Fire Safety Plans & Training, and Fire Code Compliance & Inspection Order Solutions. We focus on clear communication, practical solutions, fair pricing, and responsive service.

We understand that some fire code or inspection matters are urgent, and we aim to respond quickly when timelines are tight. Our goal is client satisfaction: helping clients understand the issue, plan the next step, and move forward with confidence.

Where engineering services are required, we provide them within the authorized scope of a Professional Engineers Ontario (PEO) Limited Licence where applicable.
